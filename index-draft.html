JP Smart Repairs Mechanical specalist and smart paint repair
JP Smart Repairs are a mechanical and smart repair specialist based in Middlesbrough. We currently do on site and mobile smart repairs. If your car has had a scuff or you're just wanting some paint work tidying up then contact us and we can arrange to come give you a free quotation for the work. All our work comes with a guarentee and because we're also mobile we can travel around the whole North East area.
We do all kinds of mechanical work ranging from the fitting of brake pads to engine work. We're also able to provide your car with it's annual service at a cost cheaper than the manufacturer. Alongside our mechnical work we have the smart repair side of the business which also includes Alloy Wheel Refurbishment.

Mechanical Work

We undertake all forms of mechanical work here at JP Smart Repairs. So if you're having problems with your car feel free to come see us and we'll be happy to take a look and provide you with an honest quote for the work we do.
