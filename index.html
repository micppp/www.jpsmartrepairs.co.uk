We specialise in SMART (Small, Medium Area Repair Technology) to repair scratches and scuffs from a cars paint work. All repairs are carried out in the garage and depending on the size should only take a few hours to repair. SMART is an efficient and cost effective way to repair your car without the car requiring a visit to a major body shop.
Alongside the SMART repair service we offer we also do mechanical work. We carry out all forms of mechanical work on all vechicles. All the work is carried out in our garage and alongside the work we also offer engine diagnostics and can carry out your servicing needs.
We also offer Alloy wheel refurbishment and can offer discounts if you are needing all four wheels refurbished. We also offer a range of colours should you fancy a change whilst getting your wheels refurbished.
